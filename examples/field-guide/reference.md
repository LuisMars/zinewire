## Poisonous Lookalikes

/two-columns

**Never** confuse these:

| Edible | Toxic lookalike |
|--------|----------------|
| Wild garlic | Lily-of-the-valley |
| Elderflower | Giant hogweed |
| Chamomile | Mayweed |
| Sweet chestnut | Horse chestnut |
| Field mushroom | Death cap |

/column

> The most important rule in foraging: **when in doubt, leave it out.** No free meal is worth a trip to A&E.

A good botanical field guide with photographs (not illustrations) is essential. Cross-reference at least two sources before eating anything new.

*Happy foraging.*
